Enterprise IMS
A Modern Inventory Management System


| About |


Enterprise IMS is a modern inventory management system designed to simplify inventory operations for businesses of all sizes. It provides an efficient way to manage products, suppliers, purchases, and sales while maintaining accurate stock records in real time. The system automatically generates professional invoices, tracks every transaction, and helps businesses monitor inventory with ease.


| Features |


  • Interactive dashboard with inventory statistics
  • Real-time stock quantity management
  • Purchase management for incoming inventory
  • Sales management for outgoing inventory
  • Automatic stock updates after every transaction
  • Printable invoices generated dynamically
  • Supplier management with transaction history
  • Inventory visualization using interactive charts
  • Secure authentication for authorized users
